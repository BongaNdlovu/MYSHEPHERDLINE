## Purpose

This manual describes records held by the responsible party and how requesters may access them under the Promotion of Access to Information Act (PAIA).

## Categories of records

- Member directory and pastoral care records
- Visit and task logs
- User account and audit records
- Operational policies and compliance records

## Request procedure

1. Submit a PAIA request to the Information Officer using the official form where applicable.
2. The Information Officer logs the request and acknowledges receipt.
3. The responsible party evaluates the request within statutory timelines.
4. A decision is communicated in writing with reasons where access is refused.
